Install BuhlOS

Get the app on your phone's home screen — fullscreen, faster, with reminders and no browser bar in the way.

✓ BuhlOS is already installed on this device.

Install on iPhone or iPad

1
Open buhlapp.xyz in Safari. The install option only works in Safari — not Chrome or Firefox on iPhone.
2
Tap the Share button in the toolbar below the page.
This is the Share icon
3
Scroll down in the Share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen".
+ Add to Home Screen
4
Tap "Add" in the top right. You'll find the BuhlOS icon on your home screen — open it from there, not Safari.
Tip: if the Share button isn't showing, tap the address bar once to reveal Safari's toolbar.

Using Chrome on iPhone? Switch to Safari first

Why? Apple doesn’t let any browser other than Safari install web apps on iPhone — not Chrome, not Firefox, not Edge. The fix is one tap: Open in Safari, then install from there.
1
In Chrome, tap the ··· menu in the top-right corner.
2
Tap "Open in Safari". The same page reopens in Safari with the same login.
3
In Safari, tap the Share button in the bottom toolbar.
This is the Share icon
4
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen", then Add.
+ Add to Home Screen
After install: always open BuhlOS from the home-screen icon, not Chrome or Safari.

Install on Android

1
Open buhlapp.xyz in Chrome. Chrome has the best install experience on Android.
2
Tap the menu (three dots ⋮) in the top-right corner of Chrome.
3
Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen" (the wording varies slightly by Android version).
4
Confirm by tapping "Install". BuhlOS will appear in your app drawer and on your home screen.
Shortcut: Chrome may also show a small "Install" prompt at the bottom of the screen when you first visit — tap it and you're done.

After installing

Always open BuhlOS from the home-screen icon — not via Safari or Chrome. The home-screen version runs fullscreen, remembers you, and is much faster.
