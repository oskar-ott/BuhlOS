<inbox-stack> S-03

A queue with a drain-it button.

Used on Overview and as the head of any inbox view. Severity raises the visual register only when the queue ages past 24h (warn) or breaches SLA (bad).

<cmd-palette> S-04

⌘K surface. Verb-first.

Reads commands from CmdRegistry. Views register their commands on mount; the palette filters fuzzy across verb + label + group. Press ⌘K anywhere on this page to open it.

<pulse-strip> S-05

Four numbers, equal weight.

At-a-glance pulse of the business. Used at the top of Overview and per-job detail. Each cell links to its queue.

<rate-flag> S-06

Yellow flag on hour anomalies.

Reviewable, not refusable. Hours over the threshold get a yellow chip + reason text. Never blocks; admin can override.

<role-pill> S-07

Subtle tints. Mono caps.

Same component as the field interface. Never a stack of three reds. Roles: admin, office, accounts, leadingHand, tradie, client.

<open-in-field> S-08

Topbar deep-link to the phone surface.

Context-aware. Knows the current job/area; opens it in a new tab on the field surface (/my-day?job=…). The fix for F-09 — never retype a URL to see what the LH sees.
